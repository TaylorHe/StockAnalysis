Story Points:5


Topic Identification

As a data analyst, I want to be able to identify topic of each Tweet, so that I can relate it to other topics

Priority:HIGH
Story Points:5


Correlate Data

Priority:HIGH
Story Points:3

Priority:MEDIUM
Story Points:4
